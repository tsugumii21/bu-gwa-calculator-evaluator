Official Bicol University Grading System & Academic Policies

Excerpts and specifications directly from the Bicol University Student Handbook (2019 Revised Edition).

Academic Grading Scale (Handbook Page 28, BOR Res. 89 s. 2006)

Grade Rating Percentage Equivalent Adjectival Rating Handbook Description
1.0 – 1.4 95 – 100% Outstanding Student has mastered all of the course's major instructional objectives.
1.5 – 1.7 92 – 94% Superior Student has mastered major objectives and almost all minor objectives.
1.8 – 2.5 84 – 91% Very Satisfactory Student has mastered major objectives and most minor objectives.
2.6 – 2.8 78 – 83% Satisfactory Student has mastered some major objectives and most minor objectives.
2.9 – 3.0 75 – 77% Fair / Passing Lowest passing mark (3.0 = 75%). Mastery of basic required objectives.
3.1 – 4.0 Below 75% Conditional Lowest possible mid-term rating. Requires remedial work.
5.0 Failure Failure Lowest possible final rating. Unearned course credit.
INC Incomplete Incomplete Incomplete course work. Excluded from GWA computation until completed. Given 1 school year to remove (Page 30).
DRP Dropped Dropped Officially dropped course within prescribed class hours. Excluded from GWA.

Graduation with Honors Criteria (Page 30)

  • Summa Cum Laude: GWA ≤ 1.2500
  • Magna Cum Laude: 1.2500 < GWA ≤ 1.4500
  • Cum Laude: 1.4500 < GWA ≤ 1.7500
  • Residence & Load: Evaluated to 4 decimal places. Must carry regular prescribed semestral load (no underload) and have no grade lower than 3.0 in any subject.

Scholastic Deficiency & Probation (Page 25)

  • 1 Failed Subject: Load in succeeding term reduced by 1 subject.
  • 2 Failed Subjects: Placed on Academic Probation. Load restricted to maximum 75% of regular load.
  • Academic Dismissal: Student under probation who obtains a failing grade, OR any student obtaining 3 or more failing grades in one term is dropped from college rolls.
